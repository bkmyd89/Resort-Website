The Humboldt Resort & Spa, offers visitors an unmatched resort experience amid the lush natural beauty of the Willamette Valley. Thoughtfully placed on 35 hillside acres, our luxury resort welcomes guests as they enter the property and drive past 7 scenic acres of estate vineyards.

Our California spa resort features 77 luxury guestrooms and 8 suites on four levels. Our spacious Willamette Valley lodging features gas fireplaces, either a terrace or balcony, and spa-like bathrooms providing a warm and inviting accommodations with high-end amenities for a memorable stay.

The Humboldt Spa boasts 12 treatment rooms, a fitness studio, indoor swimming pool, whirlpool and pool terrace to ensure you feel your best, while our The Humboldt Salon offers manicure and pedicure services.

Get a taste of authentic California wine country cuisine at JORY Restaurant & Bar, our acclaimed on-site restaurant and wine cellar.
